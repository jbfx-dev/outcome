Key Finding

UGC drives organic traffic through Google Search with a 2-day lag — when UGC views spike, Google search visitors to Outcome follow 2 days later (r=0.55). Google search visitors tripled week-over-week from 79 to 308/day, a +211% increase that coincided with the UGC scaling.

The Jul 6 spike (637K daily UGC views) aligns with new creator onboarding. Instagram generated 3.9M views but only 70 referral clicks — suggesting social video platforms don't drive direct traffic.

Conclusion

UGC works as brand awareness, not direct conversion. The value chain is: UGC exposure → brand recognition → Google search → site visit. This indirect path means UGC ROI should be measured by search traffic lift, not click-through attribution.
