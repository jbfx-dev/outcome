# OG / Meta Image Brief — World Cup 2026 Campaign Insights

## Specs

- **Dimensions:** 1200 x 630 px
- **Format:** PNG (transparent-free, solid background)
- **File name:** `meta-image.png`

## Brand Palette

| Role | Hex |
|------|-----|
| Background | `#0A0A0B` |
| Green accent | `#45E85C` |
| Purple accent | `#6F2EE4` |
| Blue accent | `#3B82F6` |
| Primary text | `#F5F8FF` |

## Typography

- **Headline:** PP Neue Corp Narrow Ultrabold
- **Body / secondary:** Geist Regular

## Layout

Left two-thirds: text content. Right third: data visual element.

### Text content (left)

1. Outcome logo (white, top-left corner)
2. Headline — "World Cup 2026" on line 1, "Campaign Insights" on line 2 (large, PP Neue Corp Narrow Ultrabold, white)
3. Subline — "$29.4M volume · 41 days · 10 findings" (Geist Regular, muted white ~60% opacity)
4. Badge pill — "10 FINDINGS" in green (`#45E85C`) border pill, uppercase, small

### Data visual (right)

A stylised mini chart or dashboard fragment that evokes analytics — options:

- A simplified line chart showing the volume trajectory (green line on dark grid)
- A confidence-score radial badge (like the 85% rings used in the dashboard)
- Abstract bar chart silhouette with green/purple/blue bars

Keep it decorative, not literal — it should read as "data-driven" at thumbnail size.

## Style Direction

Dark, premium, editorial. Match the dashboard's glass-card aesthetic. Subtle grid or dot pattern on the background for texture. No gradients louder than the accents. The image should feel like a polished executive report cover, not a marketing banner.

## Key Requirements

- Must be legible at small sizes (Slack/Twitter/iMessage previews are ~400px wide)
- No fine detail that disappears at thumbnail scale
- Outcome logo must be clearly visible
- Text hierarchy: headline dominates, subline secondary, badge tertiary
